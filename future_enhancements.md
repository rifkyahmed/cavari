# Cavari E-Commerce: Future Enhancements & Missing Features

This document outlines a roadmap for potential future enhancements to the Cavari e-commerce platform. It is categorized by the target audience (Customers vs. Administrators) and prioritizes features based on their potential impact on user experience, operational efficiency, and revenue generation.

---

## 1. Customer Experience Enhancements (Frontend)

These features focus on increasing engagement, building trust, and streamlining the purchasing journey.

### High Priority (Missing Essentials)
*   **User Accounts & Order History:** 
    *   *What:* Allow customers to create accounts to save shipping details, view past orders, track current order status, and manage their wishlist.
    *   *Why:* Essential for repeat business and reducing friction during checkout. Currently, the site operates largely on guest checkouts.
*   **Wishlist / Saved Items:**
    *   *What:* Enable users to save products they are interested in for future purchase.
    *   *Why:* High-ticket items like jewelry often require consideration time. A wishlist keeps the products top-of-mind and encourages return visits.
*   **Advanced Product Filtering & Sorting:**
    *   *What:* Expand the current category filters to include sorting by Price (Low-High/High-Low), Material (Gold, Platinum, Rose Gold), Gemstone Type, and "New Arrivals" vs. "Bestsellers."
    *   *Why:* Helps users find exactly what they are looking for faster, especially as the product catalog grows.

### Medium Priority (Engagement & Trust)
*   **Product Zoom & 360° Views:**
    *   *What:* Implement a high-resolution zoom feature on product images, or potentially 360-degree rotating views or short video clips of the jewelry.
    *   *Why:* For luxury items, seeing the fine details (prongs, facets, metal finish) is critical for online buying decisions.
*   **Ring Sizer Guide & Request Form:**
    *   *What:* A dedicated, interactive page explaining how to measure ring size, with an option to request a complimentary physical ring sizer by mail.
    *   *Why:* A major hurdle in online jewelry sales is size uncertainty. This reduces returns and increases conversion rates.
*   **Live Chat Support:**
    *   *What:* Integrate a live chat widget (e.g., Intercom, tawk.to) for immediate customer assistance during browsing or checkout.
    *   *Why:* Provides real-time reassurance for high-value purchases.

---

## 2. Administrator Enhancements (Backend)

These features focus on streamlining daily operations, improving customer service, and gaining business insights.

### High Priority (Operational Essentials)
*   **Order Management Pipeline:**
    *   *What:* Enhance the order details page to include a clear status pipeline (e.g., Pending -> Processing -> Shipped -> Delivered) with the ability to easily update statuses and automatically notify the customer (via email) at each stage.
    *   *Why:* Crucial for managing expectations and keeping operations organized.
*   **Inventory Tracking & Low Stock Alerts:**
    *   *What:* Implement automated tracking of product quantities. Send notifications to the admin when an item reaches a specific low-stock threshold.
    *   *Why:* Prevents overselling and stockouts, ensuring popular items are reordered promptly.

### Medium Priority (Marketing & Analytics)
*   **Discount Codes & Promotions Engine:**
    *   *What:* Create a system in the admin panel to generate and manage discount codes (percentage off, fixed amount, free shipping) with specific validity dates and usage limits.
    *   *Why:* Essential marketing tool for holidays, abandoned cart recovery, and VIP customer rewards.
*   **Abandoned Cart Tracking & Recovery:**
    *   *What:* Track users who add items to their cart but do not complete the purchase. Send automated reminder emails with incentives (if applicable).
    *   *Why:* One of the highest ROI features in e-commerce, recovering lost revenue.
*   **Sales Dashboard & Reporting:**
    *   *What:* Implement a visual dashboard showing key metrics: daily/monthly revenue, top-selling products, conversion rates, and average order value.
    *   *Why:* Provides actionable data to guide business decisions and marketing spend.

---

## 3. SEO & Content

*   **Blog / "The Ledger" Expansion:**
    *   *What:* Develop a dedicated blog section to share articles on jewelry care, gemstone sourcing, bespoke design stories, and style guides.
    *   *Why:* Excellent for SEO (drawing organic traffic) and establishing Cavari as an authority in the luxury jewelry space.

## Conclusion

The current iteration of the Cavari website provides a beautiful, functional foundation. Prioritizing **User Accounts** and an **Order Management Pipeline** will elevate it from a stunning storefront to a robust e-commerce platform capable of handling scale and fostering long-term customer relationships.
